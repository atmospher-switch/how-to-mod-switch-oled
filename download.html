Before You Download

System Requirements

  • Nintendo Switch (V1 model recommended for RCM access)
  • MicroSD card (32GB or larger recommended)
  • RCM jig or compatible method for payload injection
  • USB-C cable for connecting to PC
  • Windows, macOS, or Linux computer

Important Notes

  • Always backup your NAND before modifying your system
  • Keep your emuMMC separate from sysNAND
  • Never go online with pirated content
  • Follow all installation steps carefully
  • Join our community for support and updates
